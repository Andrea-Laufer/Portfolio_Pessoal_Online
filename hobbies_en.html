Favorite Anime - the list is long, but I've only included a few.

Black Butler

Synopsis: For me, the best anime in the world! With a complex narrative, lovable characters, and full of plot twists, Black Butler is the perfect mix of comedy, mystery, and revenge. My favorite character... him... the perfect butler: Sebastian Michaelis. I'm sharing with you the video of my favorite ending from the series.

Heaven Official's Blessing

Synopsis: This anime is sure to make anyone fall in love... who wouldn't fall for Huacheng, the Red Devastation? I did, haha. The anime keeps you glued to the screen, it has beautiful visuals, a wonderful soundtrack, and Huacheng... ah, Huacheng, haha. I'll share with you the famous scene of bride and groom.

Master of Diabolism

Synopsis: Master of Diabolism is a masterpiece, full of twists and turns. The anime is beautifully constructed and the story is 1000% captivating! My favorite character: Wei Wuxian, the protagonist. The anime is a wonderful mix of comedy (there's a lot of it!), action, mystery, intrigue, and a beautiful ending. I'm sharing the opening of the first season with you, which is absolutely gorgeous!

Dragon Raja

Synopsis: Dragon Raja - The Blazing Dawn is one of those anime that takes you by surprise. With beautiful visuals, well-structured characters and plot, the anime completely won me over when the 2nd season (The Mourner's Eyes) came out with this spectacular opening!
